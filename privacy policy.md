Privacy Policy

  Video Script to Prompt Converter

  This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

  Data Handling
  - The extension processes your input locally in your browser
  - When you convert a prompt, your text is sent directly to the OpenRouter API (api.openrouter.ai) to generate the enhanced video prompt
  - Your OpenRouter API key is stored locally in your browser's storage and is never transmitted to any server other than OpenRouter for authentication purposes
  - No data is collected, aggregated, or shared with third parties

  Cookies
  - This extension does not use cookies

  Children's Privacy
  - This extension is not intended for children under 13

  Changes to This Policy
  - If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with the extension

  Contact
  - For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the extension developer
